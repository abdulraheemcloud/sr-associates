What is Udyam Registration?

Udyam Registration (formerly MSME Registration) is a government registration for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises. It provides various benefits including subsidies, priority sector lending, and government tender eligibility.

Did You Know? Over 6 crore MSMEs in India contribute 30% to India's GDP. Get registered to avail benefits!

Classification Criteria:

Micro Enterprise Investment: upto ₹1 Cr | Turnover: upto ₹5 Cr
Small Enterprise Investment: upto ₹10 Cr | Turnover: upto ₹50 Cr
Medium Enterprise Investment: upto ₹50 Cr | Turnover: upto ₹250 Cr

Benefits of Udyam Registration

Collateral-free Loans

Easier access to bank loans without collateral under CGTMSE scheme

Interest Subsidy

Lower interest rates on business loans (up to 2% subsidy)

Tax Benefits

Income tax exemptions and rebates under various sections

Government Tenders

Reserved quotas and preference in government procurement

Patent & IP Support

Subsidies on patent registration and IP protection

Market Access

Access to national and international trade fairs

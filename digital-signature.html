Get Class 2 and Class 3 Digital Signature Certificate for GST, Income Tax, and MCA filing

What is Digital Signature Certificate?

Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is a secure digital key that authenticates your identity online. It is mandatory for GST registration, Income Tax e-filing, Company incorporation, and various government filings.

  • Class 2 DSC - For Income Tax & GST filing
  • Class 3 DSC - For MCA & E-Tender filing
  • Valid for 1, 2, or 3 years
  • Issued by licensed Certifying Authorities
  • Legally valid as physical signature

Note: DSC is mandatory for all company filings with MCA and for GST registration.

Documents Required:

  • PAN Card (Mandatory)
  • Aadhar Card (Mandatory)
  • Passport Size Photo
  • Address Proof (Voter ID/Passport)
  • Mobile Number & Email ID

Why GST Registration?

GST registration is mandatory for businesses with turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services). Get expert assistance for seamless registration and avoid penalties.

Benefits of GST Registration:

  • Legal recognition as a supplier of goods/services
  • Input tax credit benefits on purchases
  • Easier business expansion and interstate sales
  • Competitive advantage over non-registered businesses
  • Eligibility for government tenders

Note: Late registration can lead to penalties up to 100% of tax liability. Register on time!

Documents Required:

  • PAN Card of Business/Individual
  • Aadhar Card
  • Proof of Business Address
  • Bank Account Statement/Cancelled Cheque
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Business Registration Proof (if any)
